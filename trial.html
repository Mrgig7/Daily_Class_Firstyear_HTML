Helo Radio

Gateway to the Media-Dark regions

In the age, when the entertainment channels are in our pockets—and we are pampered with overdose of entertainment through multiple devices and mediums—there are many areas on this earth that are media dark and disconnected.

At Helo, we have utilised the ever-increasing penetration of basic mobile phones—works seamlessly on smartphones as well—and built Helo Radio that works without any internet or radio connectivity. Helo Radio provides you the power to create a robust solution that is:

  1. Simple to use
  2. Always on
  3. On-demand
  4. Free for the customer

 

Helo Groups

Power to integrate and create communities without the use of internet

Research proves the existence of a large number of areas devoid of internet connectivity and/or people devoid of technological expertise. To empower these people with the power of community communication we have developed a simple solution Helo Groups. With Helo Groups, organisations or individuals can create groups wherein the mode of delivery of information is SMS.

 

Helo Recharge

The 'Right Now' messaging for 1 billion people

Humans are genetically determined or compelled to want things — right now. It’s called instant gratification, and it’s enticing. Instant gratification is the desire to experience delight or contentment without any delay or interruption. Basically, it’s when you want it; and you want it right now.

We have built Helo Recharge platform on the 'pleasure principle'—driving force that compels human beings to gratify their needs, wants, and urges. Recharges can be offered instantly and perfectly follow 'pleasure principle'. Gratification by providing recharges can help you growth hack your marketing objectives.

 

Helo Survey

Let customers talk, measure customer satisfaction and gather actionable insights

The brands that people prefer are never permanent; Today's market leader can be easily forgotten in the years to come. It’s crucial that organisations don't loose attention. New customers are important but customer retention is the biggest challenge. Organisations spend a lot of money in customer acquisition and if the customers are not retained the profitability of the business goes into jeopardy.

Helo Surveys is an end user friendly feedback platform that uses simple products like Missed Calls, SMS and Voice to run variety of surveys depending on the objective.
